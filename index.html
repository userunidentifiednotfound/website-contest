About The Contest

Web Development Challenge

Design and develop an innovative website for the School of Engineering & Technology that showcases programs, faculty, and resources.

Team Formation

Form a team of exactly 4 members. At least one team member must have web development experience to lead the project.

Timeline

The contest runs from June 4th to June 20th, 2025. All submissions must be completed by June 19th, 11:59 PM to be eligible for judging.

Why Participate?

Showcase your web development skills
Win exciting prizes and recognition
Build your portfolio with real projects
Network with industry professionals

Selection Process

The top 20 teams will be selected for the final round to showcase their websites with detailed explanations of each element and design decision.

Final Presentation: June 20th, 2025

Contest Guidelines

Please review all guidelines carefully before registering for the competition.

1 Team Requirements

  • Each team must consist of exactly 4 members.
  • At least one team member must have web development experience.
  • All team members must be currently enrolled students.
  • Each student can only be part of one team.

2 Project Requirements

  • Website must be for the School of Engineering & Technology.
  • Site must be responsive and work on mobile devices.
  • Code must be hosted on GitHub with proper documentation.
  • Website must be deployed and accessible online.

3 Judging Criteria

  • Design & User Experience (30%)
  • Functionality & Features (25%)
  • Code Quality & Organization (20%)
  • Innovation & Creativity (15%)
  • Responsiveness & Accessibility (10%)

4 Final Round

  • Top 20 teams will be selected for the final round.
  • Teams must present their website and explain each element.
  • Presentation time: 10 minutes + 5 minutes Q&A.
  • All team members must participate in the presentation.

Important Reminder

All submissions must be completed before June 19th, 11:59 PM. Late submissions will not be accepted under any circumstances.
